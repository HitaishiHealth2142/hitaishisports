FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We recommend dressing in layers: moisture-wicking base layer, insulating mid-layer, and waterproof outer layer. Don't forget gloves, goggles, and a helmet (which we can provide). Avoid cotton as it retains moisture.

Most children can start learning snowboarding basics around age 5-6, when they have sufficient strength and coordination. We offer special kids' programs with smaller boards and fun teaching methods.

Private lessons offer one-on-one instruction tailored to your specific goals and learning pace. Group lessons (max 5 people) are more social and economical, with instruction geared to the group's average level.

During peak season, we highly recommend reserving equipment at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability of your preferred board size and style. Off-season, same-day rentals are usually available.
