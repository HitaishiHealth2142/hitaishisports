BASKETBALL TRAINING TIPS

1 Shooting Form

Master the BEEF technique (Balance, Eyes, Elbow, Follow-through) for consistent shooting. Practice form shooting daily from close range before moving back.

2 Ball Handling

Develop your weak hand by spending 70% of dribbling drills with your non-dominant hand. Try the "two-ball dribble" drill to improve coordination.

3 Defense Stance

Maintain a low, wide stance with active hands. Focus on sliding your feet rather than crossing them to maintain balance and quick direction changes.

BASKETBALL HISTORY

1891 - The Birth of Basketball

Dr. James Naismith invents basketball using a soccer ball and peach baskets.

1946 - NBA Founded

The BAA, which later becomes the NBA, is established.

1984 - Michael Jordan's Debut

Michael Jordan joins the Chicago Bulls, revolutionizing basketball globally.

1992 - Dream Team

U.S. Olympic Dream Team dominates the Barcelona Games.

2003 - LeBron James Debuts

LeBron James enters the NBA and becomes a legend.

2010 - Global Expansion

Basketball gains worldwide popularity, with stars from every continent.
