ABOUT POWERPLAY HOCKEY

Founded in 2005, PowerPlay Hockey has grown from a local training camp to the premier ice hockey development program in North America, producing 35 NHL draft picks and countless collegiate athletes.

Our state-of-the-art training facility features Olympic-sized rinks, video analysis rooms, and the latest in sports science technology to give players every competitive advantage.

OUR PROGRAMS

From beginners to professionals, we offer comprehensive hockey development programs for all ages and skill levels.

ELITE DEVELOPMENT

For competitive players ages 12-18 focused on collegiate and professional pathways.

YOUTH ACADEMY

Fundamental skill development for ages 5-12 in a fun, engaging environment.

ADULT LEAGUES

Competitive recreational leagues for all skill levels, ages 18+.
