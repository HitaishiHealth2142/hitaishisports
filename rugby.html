EQUIPMENT GUIDE

Choosing the Right Rugby Ball

Match balls are made for grip and accuracy, while training balls are more durable. Size 5 is standard for adults; juniors use size 4 or 3.

Rugby Boots & Studs

Boots with longer studs are best for soft, muddy pitches; shorter studs suit firm ground. Forwards often prefer 8-stud boots for grip in scrums.

Protective Gear

Mouthguards are essential. Scrum caps and shoulder pads offer extra protection, especially for forwards and younger players.
