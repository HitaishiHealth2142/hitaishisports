2036 Olympics

On Wednesday, Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya expressed the government’s commitment to promote traditional Indian sports on a global scale. He emphasised that a collective effort is needed to include kho kho in the Asian Games and the 2036 Olympics.

India is aiming to host the 2036 Olympics and has submitted a ‘Letter of Intent’ to the International Olympic Committee’s Future Host Commission. If India secures the hosting rights, kho kho is one of six sports that the ministry’s Mission Olympic Cell plans to recommend for inclusion in the 2036 Olympics.
