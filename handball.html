HANDBALL TRAINING TIPS

1 Shooting Technique

Master the jump shot and sidearm shot techniques for consistent scoring. Practice from different angles and distances to improve accuracy.

2 Passing Drills

Develop quick and accurate passing with partner drills. Focus on both one-handed and two-handed passes for different game situations.

3 Defensive Positioning

Learn proper defensive stance and footwork. Focus on maintaining balance while moving laterally to contain offensive players.
