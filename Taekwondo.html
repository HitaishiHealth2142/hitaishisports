    Taekwondo Styles

    WT (World Taekwondo)

    Recognized by the Olympics, WT style emphasizes full-contact sparring with electronic scoring.

    ITF (International Taekwon-Do Federation)

    Focuses on traditional forms, semi-contact sparring, and self-defense techniques.

    Taekwondo Through Time

    1945

    Taekwondo Founded

    Post-WWII Korea sees the formalization of martial art schools, leading to Taekwondo's development.

    2000

    Olympic Debut

    Taekwondo becomes an official Olympic sport at the Sydney Games.

    2016

    Global Expansion

    Taekwondo continues to grow worldwide with millions of practitioners and numerous federations.

    2024

    Paris Olympics

    Taekwondo returns to the Olympics with new events and athletes.
